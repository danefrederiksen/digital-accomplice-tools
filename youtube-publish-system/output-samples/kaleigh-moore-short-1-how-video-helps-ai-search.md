# How Does Video Help You Show Up in AI Search?

Video is how B2B brands prove they're real. AI search engines pull from human content because that's what their users trust to make buying decisions.

## Quick Answer

- B2B buyers are already using AI search engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity to research software before they buy.
- AI search engines treat human, video-based content as more trustworthy than brand-written marketing copy.
- Video signals humanity, and humanity is what wins both the buyer's trust and the LLM's citation.

## Why video moves the needle on AI search visibility

The shift is already happening. B2B buyers research software through AI search engines before they ever click on a vendor website. That changes what kind of content actually gets seen.

Brand-written marketing pages don't carry much weight in AI search. They read like sales material, and LLMs are trained to weigh community-validated, third-party signals more heavily. Video flips that script. When a real subject matter expert, a VP of product or a senior developer, talks on camera about how a problem actually gets solved, that content reads as authentic. It's harder to fake at scale and easier for AI engines to cite.

> "People like to buy from people, so I think this is what you're talking about as well. Getting people on video, leveraging your humanity in this world of AI, that is one of the most advantageous things you can do." — Kaleigh Moore

The most useful place for a B2B company to put video right now is internal voices on LinkedIn. Not the CMO. The actual experts. A VP of product explaining a tradeoff, a developer showing why a specific approach wins, a customer success lead walking through a real onboarding pattern. That kind of content earns AI visibility because it earns human trust first.

Video also gives you the most repurposable raw material in B2B. One 15-minute interview becomes shorts, audio clips, transcripts, and blog content. Every format is a fresh surface for AI search to find. If a brand is choosing one investment, video covers more ground than text alone ever can.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Are B2B buyers really using AI search to make purchase decisions?
Yes. AI search engines have become standard tools for product discovery and research, especially for software buying. Buyers use ChatGPT, Perplexity, and similar engines to compare vendors before they ever visit a website.

### Why does video matter more than written content for AI visibility?
Video proves humanity in a way text can't. AI search engines weigh community signals and third-party validation more than brand-owned written copy. A real expert on camera carries more trust than a polished blog post.

### Which employees should be on video for B2B AI visibility?
The internal subject matter experts, not the marketing team. VPs of product, senior developers, and customer-facing leads carry the most authority. Their content earns human trust first, which earns AI search citations second.
