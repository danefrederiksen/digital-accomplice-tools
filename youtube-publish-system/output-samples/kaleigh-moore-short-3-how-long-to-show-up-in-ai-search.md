# How Long Does It Take to Show Up in AI Search Results?

Plan for 6 to 9 months. AI search visibility is a long-term play, not a quick win. The brands moving today are capturing white space the rest of the market won't have access to in a year.

## Quick Answer

- AI search visibility takes 6 to 9 months of consistent investment, not a single campaign.
- LLMs are constantly retraining on new content, so brands publishing now will be cited as models update.
- White space still exists in most B2B niches, but first-mover advantage closes that window fast.

## Why this is a long-term play, not a quick win

LLMs don't index a brand the way a search engine does. They retrain on snapshots of the web, weight community signals heavily, and update what they cite as new content gets discovered. That means the work a brand publishes today shows up in AI search results months later, after the next round of model updates.

> "There's a lot of white space here that's still left to be claimed when it comes to the AI visibility and citation rate stuff. So I think video is a really smart play. Again, not a quick win, but a smart long-term investment." — Kaleigh Moore

The brands publishing consistent video content right now are seeding the next generation of LLMs. Every new interview, every short clip, every transcript is another touchpoint a model can pick up and cite the next time it retrains. The compounding effect kicks in over 6 to 9 months. Brands waiting on results in week one are measuring the wrong thing.

There's also a window here that's closing. Most B2B niches still have wide-open citation rate. Competitors haven't claimed every question, every search term, every category. That changes fast. A brand that starts now and publishes steadily for 6 months will own real estate that can't be bought back later. The brands waiting until "AI search is proven" will arrive after the slot is filled.

This is closer to a survival strategy than a marketing experiment. Models are constantly assessing new information. The work has to be ongoing to stay cited. But the brands that start today have a first-mover advantage that compounds with every model update.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How long does it take to start showing up in AI search results?
Plan for 6 to 9 months of consistent video and content publishing. LLMs retrain on new web content periodically, so the work you publish now shows up in citations as models update over the following months.

### Is AI search visibility a one-time effort or ongoing?
It's ongoing. LLMs are constantly assessing new information, so brands need to keep publishing. A brand that publishes for three months and stops will lose visibility as models retrain on fresher content from competitors.

### Why is right now the best time to start AI search optimization?
Most B2B niches still have wide-open citation rate. The brands moving today are capturing white space that won't be available in 12 to 18 months. First-mover advantage compounds with every model update.
