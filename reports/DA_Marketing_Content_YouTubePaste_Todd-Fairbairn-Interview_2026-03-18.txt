TITLE:
B2B Marketing With a 3-Person Team: What Actually Works

DESCRIPTION:
How does a 3-person B2B marketing team compete with enterprise content machines? Todd Fairbairn, Head of Marketing at Emotiv Mobility, breaks down exactly how.

Todd runs brand and marketing for an automotive supply chain company that just launched at CES. His team: one content creator, one performance marketer, and himself. In this conversation with Dane Frederiksen, they cover the real playbook for small B2B teams navigating AI, video, and the pressure to do more with less.

Key takeaway: AI handles the volume game. Video handles the trust game. Smart B2B teams use both — but lead with humanity, not automation.
