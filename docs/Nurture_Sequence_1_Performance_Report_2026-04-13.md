## Key Learnings & Recommendations

1. **8.4% reply rate validates the approach.** LinkedIn DM outreach with a value-first offer (snapshot or video interview) works. Don't overthink it.
2. **The video interview offer converts better to calls than the snapshot.** Most SQLs came after a video interview was completed or offered — the snapshot alone generated interest but fewer bookings.
3. **Follow-up cadence matters.** 134 follow-ups + 93 final nudges shows discipline. Some replies came on the 2nd or 3rd touch.
4. **Referral partners had the highest reply rate (15.4%).** These are warm by nature — lean into this channel harder.
5. **46 prospects were stuck in follow_up_2 limbo** — never marked cold, never replied. The tools need an auto-cold rule (e.g., 14+ days since last action with no reply → auto-mark cold).
6. **Round 2 should track `nurture_round: 2`** so we can compare performance between first-touch and re-approach sequences.
