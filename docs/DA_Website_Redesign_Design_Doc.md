### Origin Story (StoryBrand: The Guide — Empathy)

**Full narrative copy (from sitemap PDF):**

Once upon a time, producing video was simple — hire video experts. They did all the hard parts but charged big bucks, so video was for special occasions. Then things changed. Social media, AI and the remote work revolution made video a part of everyday life. By the end of Covid, webcams and phone footage became a normal part of B2B marketing. Because the content landscape is now so complex, you also need to rethink your marketing strategy.

Make no mistake, polished video content is still important, but so is the authentic un-varnished content. So how do you choose? What is the right video content strategy for your specific situation?

- What's the right mix of polished vs authentic content?
- How often should you post and where?
- How are you going to make AI work for you, not against you?

That's why we developed our proprietary process, to determine exactly:
- What content to make
- How to make it
- How much to spend on it
- Where to put it
- How to measure success

Finally you get the clarity you need to boost visibility, trust and stand out in a noisy marketplace... and the video production partner to make it all happen.

### Three Revolutions Section (sub-nav button)

There are Three Revolutions changing the way marketing works:
1. **The AI Revolution** — filling the feed with slop
2. **The Video Revolution** — making it easier than ever to make lots of video of all types
3. **The Business Culture Revolution** — prioritizing video and authenticity

Modern marketing rewards regularly posted video content where your target audience spends time and at the appropriate polish level. Sounds easy, right? It's not — and that's why we developed a process to address all three of these changes at once.

### "How We Work" — 4-Step Proprietary Process (StoryBrand: The Plan)

**Intro:** "We ensure measurable results with our proprietary process."

1. **Learn** — It starts with learning about your business. Things like your goals, audience, what's working and examining the marketplace conditions.
2. **Strategize** — Taking into account your capabilities, we develop a strategy to accomplish your goals. We'll explore how AI tools and strategies like "Pre-purposing" can augment or even replace other content creation activities.
3. **Prototype** — We then develop content prototypes and begin any needed training. This allows us to get early feedback and confirm strategic choices.
4. **Scale** — We then scale up your content pipeline using a mix of your internal and external resources to ensure sustainability. Regular measurement and reporting allows us to refine and adjust as market conditions evolve.

## Rules for This Build

1. **Small steps.** Never build more than one section without reviewing with Dane.
2. **No rushing.** Quality over speed. Dane said "ASAP but don't railroad it."
3. **Review at every phase.** Each phase ends with a Dane review checkpoint.
4. **Zero risk to live site.** New site is completely separate. Current digitalaccomplice.com stays untouched until new site is approved and domain is swapped.
5. **Content first, design second.** Get the words right, then make it look good.
6. **StoryBrand framework throughout.** Customer is the hero, DA is the guide. Every section answers "what's in it for the customer?"
7. **Brand standards enforced.** Inter font (not Poppins), DA Orange #F8901E, no gradients, no shadows. See canonical brand standards file.
8. **Each session = 1-2 steps max.** Avoid context drift. Save, commit, fresh start.
